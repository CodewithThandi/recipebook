Chicken Biryani
A fragrant and flavorful rice dish layered with spiced meat or vegetables, popular across South Asia.
Steps:
  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs for 1 hour.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown; set aside half for garnish.
  3. Cook marinated chicken with tomatoes and fried onions.
  4. Parboil basmati rice with whole spices.
  5. Layer chicken and rice in a pot, sprinkle saffron milk and fried onions.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander, mint, and serve hot.

Garden Salad
A fresh and crunchy salad with mixed greens, veggies, and a zesty dressing.
Steps:
  1. Wash and chop lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, and carrots.
  2. Add sliced red onions and bell peppers.
  3. Toss vegetables in a large bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Mix well and serve chilled.
Fluffy Pancakes
Light and airy pancakes, perfect for a delightful breakfast treat.
Steps:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, and melted butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; mix until just combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.
  6. Serve with syrup, fruits, or your favorite toppings.

Pilau
A spiced rice dish with meat or vegetables, popular in East African cuisine.
Steps:
  1. Saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until golden.
  2. Add pilau spices (cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves).
  3. Add meat and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add washed rice and mix well.
  6. Pour in water or broth, season with salt.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is done.

Nshima
In Zambia, we call it nshima while in other countries it is known as ugali or sadza. Nshima is literally my favourite meal of all time, and I could eat it every day!
Steps:
  1. Heat water to medium heat in a pot.
  2. Reduce heat to low and gradually whisk in cornmeal.
  3. Stir until thickened and smooth.
  4. Cover and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Pour into a serving dish.
  6. Serve with a side of vegetables or meat.
Chicken Soup
A comforting and hearty soup, perfect for cold days.
Steps:
  1. Heat chicken broth in a pot.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and simmer.
  3. Stir in shredded chicken and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve hot with crusty bread.
